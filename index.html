frequently asked questions

opening an account

You can open a new account online at any time starting July 12th, 2022.

To open an account, you must be at least 18 years of age, have a social security number and a valid state-issued ID.

Your social security number is needed to verify your identity.

You will see two small deposits and a withdrawal being made in your linked bank account within 2 business days of linking your account with Thread. This helps us verify your ownership of the account before we allow deposits or transfers.

Thread Bank accounts are FDIC insured to the legal maximum of $250,000 for each ownership category.

banking

You can access your account number in your online banking or mobile banking account, and at the bottom of all your checks. If you have trouble locating your bank account number, you can contact your local banker at 423-272-2200.

IND Sweep Program for Unit Fintech Partnerships
Through the IND Thread Deposit Sweep Program, available deposits will be “swept” (moved) into deposit accounts at one or more of our Program Banks. Cash deposits made to Program Banks are covered by Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (“FDIC”) Insurance up to $3,000,000 inclusive of deposits you may already hold at the bank in the same ownership capacity. Note: Thread Bank is not responsible for monitoring the amount of deposits in any Program Bank. For more information about the program see the Thread Sweep Disclosure.
